The False Knight

  • Dodge His Attacks:
    • The False Knight's primary attacks are charging at you and slamming his weapon down.
    • Keep moving to avoid these, and be ready to dodge or jump.
  • Attack When Safe:
    • After he completes an attack, there's a brief moment where you can strike.
    • Focus on hitting him during these openings.
  • Watch for the Ground Slam:
    • When he raises his weapon high, he’s about to slam it down.
    • Dodge the slam and get a few attacks in while he's recovering.
  • Avoid the Projectiles:
    • When he’s about to charge up and throw projectiles, stay mobile to avoid them.
    • They can deal significant damage if they hit.
  • Defeat the Armored Phase:
    • Once you reduce his health, he’ll drop his armor and become more aggressive.
    • Stay focused on dodging and attacking, as his attacks will be faster, but now he's more vulnerable.


Gruz Mother
